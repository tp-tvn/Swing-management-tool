Introduction

As a mining exploration company operating under the WHS (Mines) Regulations 2022 (WA), we are required to conduct and record daily prestart meetings, toolbox talks, and shift handovers. This tool was developed to address several consistency issues that arose from our morning prestart routine and related documents used across the swing — specifically around:

  • The preservation of context between daily meetings,
  • The continuity of records across the full span of a swing, and
  • The complete capture of safety and technical information within the end-of-swing handover document.
Purpose

This tool is a single-file web application for running and recording daily safety prestart meetings, managing swing-end handovers, and logging toolbox talks across our various projects in a uniform and standardised way. It runs entirely in your browser with no internet connection required (unless using the optional summary feature). All data is saved automatically to your device — no server, no login, no installation.

The goal is to replace word document records and disconnected email chains with one consistent, searchable record structure that allows our crews to preserve context across the span of a swing and produce a handover that is complete, saves time, and is easily exportable and presentable to the incoming team.

To start using the tool, open the Meetings.html file in your browser by pasting the file path into your address bar.

Layout

The tool is organised into six tabs across the top:

TabDescription
Today's Prestart MeetingThe main daily meeting form. Filled in at each shift prestart and saved to the swing log.
Swing LogA full archive of every saved meeting for the current swing. Meetings are listed by date and shift. Open any entry to review it in full. Open actions from across the swing are surfaced here as well.
Handover DocumentA structured end-of-swing handover covering safety, site access, earthworks, drilling, sampling, assay, survey, fuel, vehicles, and general business. Includes an executive summary generator to provide broader context.
Toolbox TalksA separate form for recording toolbox talks, including topic, presenter, attendees, and a discussion summary. Past toolbox talks are listed below the form.

At the top right of the page there are three utility buttons:

  • Sun/Moon icon — toggles light/dark mode.
  • Gear icon — opens the settings panel (see below).
  • Export / Import — backs up or restores all meeting data as a JSON file that preserves the contents of each tab.
Settings Panel

Click the gear icon to expand the settings panel. Configure this once on each device you use the tool from:

  • Prestart Recipients — email addresses (one per line) that receive the Send to Team prestart email each shift.
  • Handover Recipients — email addresses for the swing-end handover email.
  • Records Folder URL — a link to your shared drive folder.

Recipient lists are saved locally to your device only. If you use the tool on multiple devices, enter these settings on each one separately.

Main Features — Daily Prestart Meeting

The Daily Prestart Meeting tab is the main one you will use at the start of every shift. The layout logic is broadly consistent across all tabs.

Step 1 — Shift Details

Fill in the project name, date, shift (Dayshift/Nightshift), and the supervisor's name.

Step 2 — Attendance and Breathalyser

The attendance list carries over automatically from the previous meeting, so crew names will already be populated each day. For each person, confirm or update their breathalyser result (Pass/Fail), record their initials, and tick the Signed checkbox once they have physically signed the register. Rows with a Pass result display in green; Fail in red.

To add someone new, click + Add Person — they will be included in the roster from the next meeting onwards. Removing a row and saving will likewise drop that person from future lists.

Step 3 — Meeting Content
  • Previous 24hrs Performance — summary of what was achieved on the last shift. The previous meeting's daily tasks will appear as a blue carry-forward box above this field as a quick reference.
  • Daily Tasks — the planned work scope for this shift.
  • Safety Focus — today's safety focus, hazards to highlight, or toolbox talk topics covered.
  • High Risk Work — high-risk activities planned for the shift, permits required, and controls in place.
  • General Business — announcements, logistics, or any other items raised in the meeting.
Step 4 — Actions

Click + Add Action to log any follow-up items raised during the meeting. Assign each action to a person with their email address and a due date. Once complete, tick the Done checkbox — completed actions will grey out in the list.

Step 5 — Save and Send
  • Save — saves the current meeting to the swing log. Do this before leaving the prestart tab.
  • Print / PDF — opens the browser print dialog. Use this to save a PDF copy for your records folder.
  • Send to Team — opens a pre-addressed email in your default mail client with the full prestart content in the body, ready to send to your recipient list. Recipients must be configured in Settings first.
  • New Meeting — clears the form ready for the next meeting. Always save first before clicking this.
Data and Backup

All data is stored in your browser's local storage. It will persist between sessions on the same device but will be lost if you clear your browser data. It is recommended to use the Export button at the end of each swing to save a JSON backup to your records folder.

To restore data on a new device or after a browser reset, use the Import button and load the most recent backup file.
